Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2025  |  Applies to shadowlore.life

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

This Site is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. We take a minimal approach to browser storage and do not use traditional tracking cookies for advertising or analytics purposes.

What We Use

ShadowLore uses browser Local Storage (not cookies in the traditional sense) for one specific purpose:

Name Type Purpose Duration
sl_cookie_consent Local Storage Remembers whether you have accepted or declined the cookie consent notice. Used to avoid showing the banner on every page load. Until cleared by user

This data is stored only on your device. It is never transmitted to any server and is not accessible by any third party.

Third-Party Storage

The following third-party services are used on this Site and may set their own cookies or storage:

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
GitHub Pages Hosting provider. May log access data (IP address, user agent, timestamps) as part of standard server operations. View policy
Google Fonts Typeface delivery (Cinzel, Crimson Text). Google may log font requests. View policy
Unsplash CDN Image delivery. Unsplash may log image requests. View policy

Cookies We Do NOT Use

To be explicit, ShadowLore does not use:

  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Analytics tracking cookies (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Social media tracking pixels (Facebook, Twitter/X, etc.)
  • Session cookies for user authentication
  • Cross-site tracking of any kind

Your Choices

You are in full control of the storage on your device:

  • Accept or Decline: Use the cookie consent banner when you first visit the Site.
  • Clear Local Storage: Open your browser's DevTools → Application → Local Storage → select the Site domain → delete the sl_cookie_consent entry. This will reset your preference and show the consent banner again on your next visit.
  • Block all cookies: Configure your browser settings to block cookies and local storage from all sites. Note that this may affect the functionality of other websites you visit.

Manage Your Consent

You can reset your cookie consent at any time by clicking the button below. This will clear the stored preference and show the consent banner on your next page load.

European Union Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since ShadowLore does not collect personal data directly, most GDPR rights are not applicable to us. For any third-party data (GitHub, Google, Unsplash), please contact those services directly using the links in the table above.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our practices. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.
