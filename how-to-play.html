Your First Steps Into the Dark

How to Play

No experience required. The shadows will guide you.

Getting Started

Your Journey in Six Steps

ShadowLore runs on AI Dungeon — a powerful text-based narrative engine. Here's everything you need to know to begin.

  1. 1

    Create a Free Account

    Visit play.aidungeon.com and sign up for a free account. No payment is required to begin ShadowLore. Your account saves your story progress automatically.

  2. 2

    Open the Anthology

    Click the Play Now button on this site. This will take you directly to the ShadowLore Anthology scenario on AI Dungeon. No searching required — you'll arrive at the opening of the first tale.

  3. 3

    Choose Your Tale

    The Anthology opens with a selection screen. You can begin with The Hollow King (recommended for first-time players), or dive into any chapter that calls to you. Each tale is self-contained but rewards players who explore multiple chapters.

  4. 4

    Read and Respond

    The story unfolds in text passages. When the narrative pauses, it's your turn. Type what your character does, says, or thinks — in plain English. You can be as detailed or as brief as you like. The AI will continue the story based on your input.

  5. 5

    Make Choices That Matter

    ShadowLore is built around consequence. Sparing an enemy, revealing a secret, or staying silent — every choice shapes what comes next. There are no wrong answers, only different paths through the dark. Some choices close doors. Some open gates into places you didn't know you wanted to find.

  6. 6

    Explore Every Shadow

    When you reach an ending, consider starting again with different choices. The Anthology was designed for multiple playthroughs. The Archivist has noted at least 14 distinct conclusions across the three chapters — most players find 2 or 3 on their first run.

Archivist's Tips

  • Type complete sentences for richer story responses. "I draw my blade and step forward" yields more than "attack".
  • You can ask the narrator questions by typing "I ask about..." — some characters hold secrets they'll only share if prompted directly.
  • If the story goes in an unexpected direction, use the Undo or Retry buttons in AI Dungeon to try a different phrasing.
  • The world of Velundra has its own rules. Attempting actions that violate the lore (e.g. using modern technology) will result in interesting narrative pushback.
  • Save your story frequently using AI Dungeon's built-in save feature — a good tale is worth preserving.
  • Read slowly. The prose is dense by design. The details you skim past may matter three scenes later.
  • Trust your instincts. The "good" choice and the "right" choice are rarely the same thing in ShadowLore.

Questions

Common Questions

Is ShadowLore free to play?

Yes. A free AI Dungeon account is sufficient to access and complete the full Anthology. Premium AI Dungeon features may enhance the experience but are not required.

How long is each chapter?

Each chapter is designed for 30–90 minutes of play depending on how deeply you explore. Completionists who seek all paths may spend several hours per chapter.

Is the content suitable for everyone?

ShadowLore contains dark themes including mortality, moral ambiguity, and psychological horror. It is intended for readers aged 16 and above. Content is literary, not graphic.

Can I share my story?

Yes. AI Dungeon allows you to share story transcripts. We encourage players to share notable moments and alternate endings with the community.

Will more chapters be added?

Yes. The Anthology is a living project. New chapters are written and released periodically. Follow this site for announcements about new tales.

What if the AI writes something off-tone?

AI Dungeon's output can occasionally drift from the established tone. Use the Retry button to generate a fresh continuation, or gently steer the narrative back with your next input.
